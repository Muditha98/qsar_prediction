Discover the Toxicity of Chemical Compounds

Predicting chemical toxicity is essential for ensuring safety in various industries like pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and environmental protection. Our web app leverages advanced machine learning techniques and molecular descriptors to estimate the toxicity levels of chemical compounds.

Molecular descriptors such as CIC0, SM1_DzZ, GATS1i, NdsCH, NdssC, and MLOGP provide key insights into chemical structure and behavior. These descriptors are fed into our model to accurately predict the toxicity levels of different compounds, helping researchers and scientists make more informed decisions.
